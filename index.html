Welcome to EZtrack

Problem

Diabetes is a serious disease and an epidemic problem. Diabetes can lead to many other health risks such as heart disease, stroke, kidney failure, premature death, etc.

Solution

We want to provide an easy way for those with diabetes to keep track of their blood sugar and health.

Easy to Use

EZtrack will be both a device and mobile app.

The device would be in the form of a bracelet. It will take a small amount of blood from your body in order calculate the results. This device is needle free, instead it sucks blood into a small container or barrel where it will measure your sugar level. The device will be automatic so you will never have to worry or forget.

Once the results are sent to your device it will alert you if your sugar level needs to be brought to your attention. The app will also have reminders and suggestions in order for you to stay on top of your health.
